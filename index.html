2100

Mindful technology.

2100 is a sociotechnic design consultancy.

We help anticipate, unpack, and shape the social consequences of emerging technology.

We critically examine the values, models, and assumptions shaping the development of technology, along with its long-term potentialities.

We help guide the commercialization of technology towards avoiding unintended harms, leaving a positive legacy, and considering a wider set of strategic options.

The impetus of this work came from 2100 Foundation, a project to recast academic research across a range of disciplines to support the decision making of technologists.

In the past, our team has done technology strategy, human-centered design, and strategic foresight projects with an array of companies incl. Ford, FedEx, Deloitte, J&J, Suncor, SAP, and BlackBerry.
