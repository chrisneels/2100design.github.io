WHO AM I?

I'm Chris.

I worked as a tech and innovation consultant for 7 years, advising companies across a range of industries on strategy, human-centered design, and agile development.

It dawned on me that we're commercializing emerging technologies with limited consideration of the future.

We build faster than we understand effects. When negative effects become clear, they're often irreversible. We become stuck with them.

I did postgraduate research at University College London to better understand this problem. My studies were interdisciplinary – drawing from STS, anthropology, sociology, philosophy, and history.

Now, I'm combining this academic knowledge with my industry experience to help you shape the effects of your technology.


Your larger asks draw from a network of social scientists, futurists, innovation strategists, designers, and developers.
